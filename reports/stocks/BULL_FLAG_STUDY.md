# Bull-flag pullback / entry / leg study (272 confirmed flags, 10 names)

swing_pivots(width=5); net of 5.0bps. Conditioned on flags that confirmed.

## The usual bull-flag pullback

- Pole gain: median **15.2%**
- **Flag depth (drop from pole high): Q1 3.8% / median 4.8% / Q3 6.6%**
- Retrace of the pole: median **0.39**; flag length median 5 bars
- => place the entry just above ~the median flag low (≈ 4.8% below the pole high).

## Entry: flag low vs breakout (forward to run top)

| entry                           |   mean% |   median% |   win% |
|:--------------------------------|--------:|----------:|-------:|
| at FLAG LOW (anticipate bottom) |    15.4 |      11.8 |    100 |
| at BREAKOUT (confirmed)         |     7.3 |       4.3 |     97 |

## Exit / re-entry

- **Run top signature** (where to exit): RSI median **69**, mr_score median **-0.89** (overbought/extended).
- **Next pullback** after the top: median **7.3%**, run resumes higher **58%** of the time, leg-2 median **9.6%** => rebuy the dip if shallow.

## Exit at first top vs HOLD through vs swing (n=271 flags with a 2nd leg)

| strategy                             |   mean% |   median% |   win% |
|:-------------------------------------|--------:|----------:|-------:|
| EXIT at first top                    |    7.31 |      4.28 |  96.68 |
| HOLD through pullback (to leg-2 top) |   10.62 |      6.55 |  85.98 |
| SWING (sell top/rebuy dip)           |   22.18 |     15.53 |  99.63 |

HOLD-through beats exit-at-first-top in **58%** of cases; SWING (perfect) beats HOLD in **100%** (but needs perfect timing).

_Entering at the flag low captures more but assumes you call the bottom; in real time gate it with oversold/5m-VWAP confirmation, or take the safer breakout entry._
